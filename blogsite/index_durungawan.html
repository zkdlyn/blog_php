Menu

Durungawan is a progression of rare and beautiful ingredients where texture, flavour and harmony is paramount.

Inspired by nature, Chef Margot's creative process begins in working closely with the farmers, fishermen, producers and artisans who cultivate produce exclusively for Durungawan. For each dish, the growth of every element and the selection of every ceramic piece are carefully considered and crafted for its role in the dining experience.
